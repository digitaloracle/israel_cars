Privacy Policy – Israel Vehicle Lookup

Last updated: March 2026

What data is stored

The extension saves the last 5 license plate numbers you searched to Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local), solely to show your recent searches when you reopen the extension. This data never leaves your device.

What data is NOT collected

  • No personal information is collected or transmitted to the developer.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • No data is shared with any third party.

External API calls

When you search for a vehicle, the extension sends the license plate number directly to Israel's open government data portal (data.gov.il). This is a public API operated by the Israeli government. The developer has no access to these requests and stores no record of them.
